PRo3D Getting Started Technology



PRo3D, short for Planetary Robotics 3D Viewer, is an interactive 3D visualization tool to allow planetary scientists to work with high resolution 3D reconstructions of the Martian surface. These reconstructions stem from orbiter images and rover images and are provided by Joanneum Research, while the viewer itself is developed at VRVis Vienna

Who uses PRo3D?
